Frequently Asked Questions

How do I merge PDFs?

Use the "Merge PDF" tool from the home screen. Select files, adjust order, and tap "Merge".

Where are my files saved?

Files are usually saved in the /Download folder. You can open them via the "Recent Files" tab.

Why is image compression taking time?

Compression time depends on image size and chosen quality. Larger images take more time.

Is internet required for all tools?

No, most tools work fully offline. Only legal pages or updates need internet.
